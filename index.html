FAQ'S

What is our goal as Green Wavers

Our goal as Green Wavers is to help counter the effects of climate change by planting trees for by doing the same, we will be able to help maintain our planet as it is and also safeguard the resources for the future generation.

How can I be part of the project to help solve the effects of Climate change?

You can be part of the program by joining the Green Wavers competition which involves a tree planting tournament whereby the winner will receive an award. You have to take a picture with every tree that you plant and upload the same to our website.

What is Climate change?

Climate change is the change in the climate of a place over a period of time. Most of it has been brought up by human activities taking place on a daily basis that harm the environment. Examples of the same include industrialisation that leads to emission of smoke ,the burning of litter, cutting down of trees etc.
